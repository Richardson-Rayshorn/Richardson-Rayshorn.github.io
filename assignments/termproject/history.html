HISTORY

We have been around since 1993 and have served many Cyclist around the world. Founded by Martin Mike Thomas and his wife Mirielle Thomas. With a passion for bicycles and a business diploma form Bozeman College, they started this small business that has grown into a massive support organization for cyclist visiting and living in the Bozeman area.
