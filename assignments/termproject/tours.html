TOURS

Rave Bicycle Tour

This tour takes you up the mountain side with the ocean breeze hitting you as you ride. The ride is 4 hours long, experience is required as you travel through the windy paths and long swinding roads.

  • COST: $35 per person
  • LOCATION: Ravine Heights

Family Bicycle Tour

Take your family on a Bicycle tour around the country side and feel the cool air as you make your way around the farm lands. The ride is 6 hours long, no experience is required as you travel through there will be support for all members of the family with plenty of fluids and rest.

  • COST: $20 per Adult, $10 for under 18
  • LOCATION: Ravine Heights
